Rules
  1. Click the "POWER"" button to turn the console ON.
  2. Press the PLAY button. Simon will give only the first signal.
  3. Repeat Simon's signal by clicking on the section the lit up. If you get the sequence (one flash) right, your level will increase by 1 and Simon will then add another flash to the sequence.
  4. Continue repeating Simon's previous sequence. Try to reach level 20!
  5. If you make an error, you'll get the "X-X" notification. Simon will then repeat the sequence so you can try again.
  6. If "STRICT" is switched on, when you make an error, the game will start again from level 1.
  7. To start the game yourself, click the reset button during the player turn.
  8. For and extra challenge, hold the ctrl button to reveal the "HARD" option.
    Note: once you have a "Hard" flash in your sequence, you won't be able to turn hard mode off until you reset to level 1
